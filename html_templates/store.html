Our Flagship Space

The Gallery, SoHo

A curated sensory journey designed for the modern individual.

Located in the heart of New York's historic SoHo district, our flagship space is more than a store. It is a dialogue between architecture, textile, and community. We invite you to experience our collections in an environment that celebrates the beauty of slow living and intentional design.
